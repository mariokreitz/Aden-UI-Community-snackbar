Community Workbench

Build, test, and preview your components in a native Aden UI environment before submitting.

01

Primary Workspace

Your main development area is located at:

src/app/contributions/my-contribution/

Edit the HTML, SCSS, and TS. Your work is already wrapped in the playground and visible in Lab Mode.

02

Multiple Submissions

To create a new, separate element, run:

ng generate component contributions/your-name

To view it, simply replace the selector tag inside:

src/app/pages/workbench/workbench.component.html
!

Design Guidelines

  • Playground: Always keep your component wrapped in <app-ui-playground>.
  • Meta Data: Fill out the meta object in your TS file (this generates the header).
